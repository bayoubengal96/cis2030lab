Frequently Asked Questions

How often are the Winter Olympics held?

They are held every four years, similar to the Summer Olympics (but on a different schedule than the Summer Games).

What kinds of sports are in the Winter Olympics?

Winter Olympics sports are ice and snow sports like skiing, snowboarding, ice hockey, figure skating, speed skating, luge, bobsled, and more.

How are countries ranked in your “Top 5” list?

I used a weighted medal system: 3 points for gold, 2 for silver, and 1 for bronze. This puts more emphasis on gold medals.

What is one “behind the scenes” fact you think is most interesting?

Luge is insanely fast—athletes can go over 80 mph on ice tracks. That’s wild considering how small the sled is.
