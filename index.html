The Winter Olympics are an international sports competition held every four years featuring snow and ice events like skiing, skating, hockey, and bobsled. I like watching because the events are fast, the athletes are incredible, and it’s fun seeing different countries dominate different sports. This site shares a quick introduction to the Winter Olympics, my top five countries, and some behind-the-scenes facts.
