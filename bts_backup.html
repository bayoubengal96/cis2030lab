Fun Facts & Trivia

  • The Winter Olympics were first held in 1924 in Chamonix, France, and were originally considered a side event to the Summer Games.
  • Norway is the most successful nation in Winter Olympics history, with more medals than any other country overall.
  • The Winter Olympics feature some of the fastest sports on Earth—luge athletes can exceed 80 mph (130 km/h) on icy tracks.
  • Ice hockey is one of the most watched Winter Olympic events, and Canada has won more Olympic ice hockey gold medals than any other nation.
